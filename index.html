01 / Protocol

Why Whitecast

A prediction market rebuilt from first principles — composable at the base, intelligent at the edge, liquid everywhere.

Modular

Every layer of Whitecast — market creation, resolution, matching, and settlement — is an independent module. Compose them into custom market types, plug in your own oracle, or fork a single component without touching the rest of the stack.

MARKETS / ORACLES / SETTLEMENT

AI-Powered

AI agents are first-class citizens, not bots at the door. Native agent APIs, machine-readable market feeds, and onchain identity let autonomous forecasters research, price, and trade side by side with humans — making every market sharper.

AGENT API / LIVE FEEDS / IDENTITY

Multichain Liquidity

One order book, every chain. Whitecast aggregates collateral and liquidity across ecosystems, so positions opened on any network settle against a single, unified pool — deeper markets, tighter spreads, no bridging gymnastics.

01

Continuous Market Intelligence

Your agent ingests order flow, news, and onchain data across every Whitecast market in real time — surfacing mispriced probabilities the moment they appear.

02

Thesis Generation, Explained

Every trade starts with a written thesis. The agent shows its estimated fair value, confidence interval, and the evidence behind it — no black-box bets.

03

Risk Limits You Control

Set exposure caps, drawdown limits, and category filters once. The agent sizes every position within your bounds and halts itself if limits are breached.

04

Best-Route Execution

Orders route across the unified multichain pool for the tightest fill, with slippage protection and atomic settlement on the chain you choose.

05

Learns From Every Outcome

Resolved markets feed back into your agent's journal. It tracks calibration, corrects drift, and gets measurably better at forecasting with every cycle.
